Simple, effective workouts for every fitness level — no gym required!

Popular Exercises

Pick an exercise and start your journey today. Consistency is the key!

Beginner

Push-Ups

A classic upper-body exercise that targets your chest, shoulders, and triceps. Perfect for building strength anywhere, anytime.

  • ⏱️ 3 sets × 15 reps
  • 🔥 Calories: ~50/session
  • 💪 Chest · Shoulders · Triceps
How to do it:
  1. Start in plank position, hands shoulder-width apart.
  2. Lower your chest toward the floor.
  3. Push back up to starting position.
Beginner

Squats

The king of lower-body exercises. Squats build powerful legs and glutes while improving your balance and core stability.

  • ⏱️ 3 sets × 20 reps
  • 🔥 Calories: ~70/session
  • 💪 Quads · Glutes · Hamstrings
How to do it:
  1. Stand with feet shoulder-width apart.
  2. Lower yourself as if sitting on a chair.
  3. Keep your back straight, push through heels to rise.
Intermediate

Plank

One of the best core-strengthening exercises. Hold the plank position to build a rock-solid core and improve posture.

  • ⏱️ 3 sets × 30–60 sec
  • 🔥 Calories: ~30/session
  • 💪 Core · Shoulders · Back
How to do it:
  1. Get into forearm plank position.
  2. Keep your body in a straight line head to toe.
  3. Hold for as long as you can without dropping hips.
Beginner

Jumping Jacks

A fun full-body cardio exercise that gets your heart pumping and your whole body warmed up in minutes.

  • ⏱️ 3 sets × 30 reps
  • 🔥 Calories: ~60/session
  • 💪 Full Body · Cardio
How to do it:
  1. Stand straight with arms at sides.
  2. Jump while spreading legs and raising arms overhead.
  3. Jump back to starting position and repeat.
Intermediate

Lunges

Lunges improve balance, coordination and leg strength while targeting multiple muscle groups simultaneously.

  • ⏱️ 3 sets × 12 reps each leg
  • 🔥 Calories: ~55/session
  • 💪 Quads · Glutes · Calves
How to do it:
  1. Step one foot forward into a wide stance.
  2. Lower your back knee toward the floor.
  3. Push back up and alternate legs.
Advanced

Mountain Climbers

A high-intensity exercise that combines core strengthening with cardiovascular conditioning for maximum results.

  • ⏱️ 3 sets × 30 sec
  • 🔥 Calories: ~80/session
  • 💪 Core · Legs · Shoulders
How to do it:
  1. Start in push-up position.
  2. Drive one knee toward your chest rapidly.
  3. Alternate legs as fast as you can.
💡
Pro Tip: Always warm up for 5 minutes before exercising and cool down after. Drink water throughout your workout!
