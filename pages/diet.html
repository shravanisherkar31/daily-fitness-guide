Healthy Diet Guide

Fuel your body with the right nutrition. Simple tips for a healthier, happier you!

Healthy Foods

A balanced diet includes a variety of whole, minimally processed foods that nourish your body.

Broccoli

Rich in fiber, vitamins C & K, and antioxidants. Great for digestion and immunity.

Carrots

Packed with beta-carotene for healthy eyesight and glowing skin.

Olive Oil

Heart-healthy fats that reduce inflammation and support brain function.

Avocado

Loaded with healthy monounsaturated fats, potassium, and fiber.

Protein Foods

Protein builds and repairs muscles, keeps you full longer, and supports a healthy metabolism.

Eggs

Complete protein with all essential amino acids. Great for breakfast to keep you full.

Fish (Salmon)

High in protein and omega-3 fatty acids. Excellent for heart and brain health.

Lentils & Beans

Plant-based protein powerhouses full of fiber, iron, and essential nutrients.

Chicken Breast

Lean protein source that supports muscle growth and keeps calories in check.

Fruits & Vegetables

Eat a rainbow of colors every day for vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants your body needs.

Banana

Quick energy boost with natural sugars, potassium, and B6 vitamins.

Orange

Vitamin C powerhouse that boosts immunity and keeps your skin healthy.

Grapes

Rich in antioxidants, resveratrol and vitamins that protect your cells.

Strawberries

Low in calories, high in vitamin C, fiber, and disease-fighting antioxidants.

Water – Your Body's Best Friend

Your body is about 60% water. Staying hydrated is one of the most important things you can do for your health!

  • Drink at least 8 glasses (2 liters) of water per day
  • Start your morning with a glass of warm water
  • Drink before, during, and after workouts
  • Add lemon or cucumber for a refreshing twist
  • Use a water tracker app to build the habit

Ideal Meal Timing

When you eat matters almost as much as what you eat!

7:00 AM Breakfast – Your most important meal
10:00 AM Mid-Morning Snack – Fruit or nuts
1:00 PM Lunch – Balanced & filling
4:00 PM Evening Snack – Light & healthy
7:00 PM Dinner – Light, 2–3 hrs before sleep
